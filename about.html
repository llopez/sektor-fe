About

Sektor is a site created under a simple idea, to help people find his favourite music easily.

How does it work?

Sektor collects and process the data then it shows that data to you on a prettified way.

How much does it cost?

Sektor has been built with open source technologies and for that reason it is totally free.

Where is sektor?

Sektor lives on the internet, more precisely behind a very very cheap infrastructure, so you have to be patient when you ask sektor for something.
